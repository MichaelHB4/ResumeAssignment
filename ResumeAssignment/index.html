Birth Sisters Doula Rack Card

For my Client Media Relations class, we were tasked with creating four deliverable products for our client. My groups client was Birth Sisters Doula, and we decided to split up each deliverable. I was tasked with creating the Rack card that would serve to inform potential clients of Birth Sisters Doula. We went through several versions and ultimately ended up with this design. The waves on the bottom of the card ended up resembling pregnant stomachs which the client felt was very appropriate for their business. The waves were then added to some of the other deliverables to create some uniformity.
